I made this project for the NASA space apps challenge: Make a MoonQuake 2.0

It was made over a period of 48 hours, and we used real data provided by NASA!

The red dots represent seismic activity on the moon, and they are located on the actual location of where they happened.

I also included a toggle to see where the apollo missions landed.

We also included the seismic waveforms created by the moonquakes, and changed the pitch so they could be heard by humans.
